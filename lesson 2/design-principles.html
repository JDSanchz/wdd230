Design Principles

Website Examples

Visual Hierarchy

Google Store

store.google.com

The Google Store uses Visual Hierarchy by giving us the most important piece of information right in the middle. Even though our "h1" is under what could possibly be a "h3" when it comes to sizes, It works because it follow the law of thirds.

PARC: Contrast

Angel Studios

angel.com

The Angel Studios Website for The Chosen gives us beautiful contrasts as you scroll down. It combines text and image together while keeping the text readable. The buttons also convey a message due to the difference in colors/contrast one being white and the other one black.

Fitt's Law

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org

The Church of Jesus Christ website is a great example of this law. Instead of just having text that links to an article the user can click on the picture being display behind the title of the article you might be interested in reading. The church logo is a big image that you can also click on and take you to the home page.
