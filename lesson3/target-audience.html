Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

Our Audience

The audience for this chamber includes local business owners and managers, as well as individuals and organizations involved in economic development and tourism in the area. The chamber may also engage with local government officials and representatives, as well as members of the community at large who have an interest in supporting and promoting the local business community. The audience would be diverse, including small and medium-sized businesses, entrepreneurs, start-ups, and large corporations, as well as professionals from different fields, students, and other members of the community who want to be informed about the economic situation and developments in the area.

Persona #1

Name: Serge Davis

Age: 20

Occupation: Full-time student

Michael is a 20-year-old full-time student currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at Brigham Young University-Idaho. He is a driven and ambitious individual with a strong interest in technology and programming. Michael is a first-generation college student and comes from a lower-income family. He is on a tight budget, but he wants to make the most of his college experience and get involved in the community. He is single and lives on campus. Michael would use the chamber of commerce site to learn more about job and internship opportunities in the area that align with his field of study. He would also be interested in finding out about any events or activities happening in the community that he could get involved in. He would also use the chamber of commerce site to stay informed about local economic development and business-related news that could impact his future job prospects after graduation.

Persona #2

Name: Sarah Johnson

Age: 32

Occupation: Small business owner

Sarah is a 32-year-old small business owner who recently moved to Rexburg, Idaho from a larger city to open her own store. She is a highly organized and ambitious individual who is passionate about her business. Sarah would use the chamber of commerce site to learn more about the local business community and to connect with other small business owners in the area. She would be interested in finding out about local events and networking opportunities where she can promote her business and connect with potential customers.

Scenario #1

Mark, a real estate investor, is looking to invest in commercial properties in Rexburg, Idaho. He visits the chamber of commerce site to find out more about the local economy and real estate market, and to contact local real estate agents or developers.

Scenario #2

Susan, a tourist, is planning a trip to Rexburg, Idaho and wants to find out more about the local attractions, restaurants and hotels. She visits the chamber of commerce site to learn more about the area and to plan her trip.

Scenario #3

John, a local resident, is interested in finding out more about the local business community and how he can support it. He visits the chamber of commerce site to read about local businesses and to find out about any volunteer opportunities he can get involved in.

Scenario #4

Michael, a college student, is looking for internships and job opportunities in his field of study. He visits the chamber of commerce site to search for job listings and to find out about any resources or programs the chamber offers to help students connect with local businesses.

Scenario #5

Sarah, a small business owner, wants to find out about upcoming networking events and trade shows in the area where she can promote her business and connect with potential customers. She visits the chamber of commerce site to find out about upcoming events and to register for them.
